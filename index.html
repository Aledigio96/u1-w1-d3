Focaccia whit Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

image:Sally Vegas

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberries Recipe That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/3 cups (350h) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 cup extra virgil olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellows onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for the brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off whit a paring knife if desired,thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bwl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
